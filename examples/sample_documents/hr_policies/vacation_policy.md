# Vacation Policy

## Overview

This document outlines the company's vacation policy for all employees.

## Eligibility

All full-time employees are eligible for paid vacation time. Employees must complete 90 days of employment before being eligible to take vacation.

## Vacation Accrual

### New Employees (0-2 years)

New employees accrue 15 days of vacation per year, which is equivalent to 1.25 days per month.

### Experienced Employees (2-5 years)

Employees with 2-5 years of service accrue 20 days of vacation per year, equivalent to 1.67 days per month.

### Senior Employees (5+ years)

Employees with more than 5 years of service accrue 25 days of vacation per year, equivalent to 2.08 days per month.

## Using Vacation Time

Employees must request vacation time at least 2 weeks in advance through the HR portal. Requests are subject to manager approval based on business needs.

Vacation days cannot be carried over to the next calendar year, except in special circumstances approved by HR.

## Unused Vacation

Unused vacation days will be paid out upon termination of employment, subject to local labor laws.
